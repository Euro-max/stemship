What is Math Gems Festoon?

Math Gems Festoon is a great opportunity for those wanting to sharpen their problem-solving skills by introducing carefully chosen sets of problems, ranging in difficulty from newcomers level to that of world-class solvers.

Nevertheless, the Math Gems Festoon not only includes weekly problem sets but also daily selections that help you keep the flame up.

Can anyone solve the problems?

Problems range widely in difficulty, from basic algebra to challenging math contests questions. However, the problems are designed to require minimal prior knowledge when possible and focus more on knowing how to apply basic tools creatively to get unexpected results. While you may not be able to solve all problems in your first visit, you will certainly improve after trying many!

How can I see my improvement?

Our score-leaderboard system also offers you the opportunity to see how you rank amongst others and observe your improvement over time.

Detailed solutions for our problems are published afterwards to ensure that everyone benefits from both his trial and the solution elegance. We also share collections of textbooks and references giving you the essential tools in your journey.

Where can I find more problem-solving sources?
